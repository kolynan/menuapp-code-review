### Section 4 — Out-of-scope risks

- `activeOrders` at 3575-3584 drops fully closed orders before grouping, so Completed cannot represent fully closed hall tables without a broader model change.
- `tableMap` at 3375-3376 strips table records down to `{ name, zone_name }`, so any future `Table.status`-based UI will silently have no data.
- `closeSession()` still uses `Promise.all(Order.update)` at helper 165-170, which is a parallel-write hotspot in a screen already carrying 429 mitigations elsewhere.
- Notification logic forces `invalidateQueries(['orders'])` at 4062 while orders are also polled every 5s at 3498.
- There is a live `console.error("Error loading guests batch:", err);` at 3651.
